# Goalie Card & CIC: Contract Form Templates & Legal Provisions

## 1. The "Portnoy-Style" $1 Buyback Provision (Side-Letter)
This is the most critical provision for maintaining the brand's soul. It triggers an option for **CIC (Coalition of Independent Coaches)** to repurchase the asset for a nominal fee ($1.00 USD).

### Section 4.5: Reversion of Assets upon Strategic Divergence
> "In the event that the Acquiring Entity (Buyer) ceases to maintain the Core Brand Values as defined in Exhibit A (e.g., 'Radical Accessibility' or 'Independent Mandate'), or if the Acquiring Entity elects to 'Sunset' or permanently dissolve the Goalie Card product or branding, CIC (The Seller) shall have the irrevocable right, but not the obligation, to repurchase 100% of the Intellectual Property, Brand, and User Data for the nominal sum of $1.00 USD."

---

## 2. Right of First Refusal (ROFR) for CIC
If a third party makes an offer to buy the Goalie Card asset from the Buyer, CIC has a 30-day window to match the offer and reclaim the company.

### Section 8.2: Right of First Refusal (ROFR)
> "Before the Acquiring Entity accepts any offer from a third party for the sale or transfer of more than 50% of the Goalie Card assets or voting stock, the Acquiring Entity shall first offer said assets to CIC under the same terms and conditions as the third-party offer. CIC shall have thirty (30) days to exercise its Right of First Refusal."

---

## 3. "Key Man" Termination Protection
PE firms often fire the founder after 18-24 months to save on salary. This clause ensures that IF they fire you, the price to buy the company back drops significantly.

### Section 12.1: Founder Protective Termination
> "If the Founder is terminated without Cause within thirty-six (36) months of the closing date, CIC's option to buy back the assets for 50% of the original sale price shall be immediately exercisable. Alternatively, if the Buyer enters bankruptcy or insolvency, the $1 Buyback Provision (Section 4.5) shall become immediately effective."

---

## 4. IP Sovereignty (The "Codebase Anchor")
Goalie Card’s value is in its AI models and database structure. We must ensure that the IP stays "clean" for the founder.

### Provision 16.4: Exclusive License Back
> "Notwithstanding any sale of the Assets, CIC shall retain a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to the underlying Source Code and Data Models for use in secondary sport verticals (e.g., Tennis, Golf, Racing) provided that such use does not directly compete with the primary Hockey Goalie vertical for a period of [X] years."
