# Goalie Card: Financial Projections (Year 1 - Year 4)

## Overview
This forecast models the transition from a service-heavy solo coaching business into a scalable SaaS platform. The core assumptions are built upon the pricing strategy laid out in `PRICING_STRATEGY.md`, assuming reasonable but aggressive month-over-month growth, and factoring in the expansion into secondary sports (Tennis/Golf) in Year 3.

---

## Year 1: Product-Market Fit & The Transition
**Primary Goal:** Stabilize local revenue with the Hybrid Retainer while beta testing the $15/mo SaaS and $99/mo Coach tiers. Achieve "default alive" status as a solo founder.

### Projected Revenue: $84,000
- **Hybrid Retainer ($300/mo):** 15 local goalies = $4,500/mo ($54k annually)
- **B2C SaaS ($15/mo):** Ramp up to 200 purely digital goalies = $3k/mo ($18k annually)
- **B2B Coaches ($99/mo):** Ramp up to 20 private coaches = $2k/mo ($12k annually)

### Projected Expenses: $12,400
- **Infrastructure:** Vercel, Supabase, AI APIs (~$200/mo) = $2,400
- **COGS (Stripe Processing 3%):** $2,500
- **Legal/Marketing/Admin:** LLC filings, beta advertising = $7,500

### Year 1 Net Profit (Founder Pay): ~$71,600

---

## Year 2: The B2B Push & National Expansion
**Primary Goal:** The software is flawless. The focus shifts entirely from local physical coaching to acquiring independent skills coaches nationwide who bring their rosters onto the platform.

### Projected Revenue: $372,000
- **Hybrid Retainer ($300/mo):** Capped at 20 local goalies MAX (Founder's time limit) = $72k annually
- **B2C SaaS ($15/mo):** 1,000 purely digital goalies by Q4 = $15k/mo ($180k annually runway)
- **B2B Coaches ($99/mo):** 100 private coaches = $10k/mo ($120k annually runway)

### Projected Expenses: $103,000
- **Infrastructure:** Increased DB and AI usage (~$1k/mo) = $12,000
- **COGS (Stripe Processing 3%):** $11,000
- **Contractors/Dev Support:** Outsourcing minor features or design updates = $50,000
- **Marketing & CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** $30,000

### Year 2 Net Profit: ~$269,000

---

## Year 3: Enterprise & Multi-Sport Expansion
**Primary Goal:** Pitch customized Enterprise deals based on two years of data. Spin off the underlying "Card" technology for Tennis/Golf and release the generic versions. Hire the first full-time employee.

### Projected Revenue: $1,050,000
- **Hybrid Retainer:** Phased out entirely; Founder shifts 100% time to CEO/Sales. (Optional $50k left for high-ticket clients)
- **B2C SaaS ($15/mo) [Multi-Sport]:** 3,000 users = $45k/mo ($540k annually runway)
- **B2B Coaches ($99/mo) [Multi-Sport]:** 300 coaches = $30k/mo ($360k annually runway)
- **Enterprise Contracts:** 3 Club Deals ($20k ea), 1 Pro Team Pilot ($40k) = $100k annually

### Projected Expenses: $366,000
- **Infrastructure:** Robust scaling and backup DB instances (~$3k/mo) = $36,000
- **COGS (Stripe Processing 3%):** $30,000
- **Personnel:** 1 Full-Stack Engineer + 1 B2B Account Executive = $240,000
- **Marketing, Travel & Legal:** Traveling to pitch Enterprise deals = $60,000

### Year 3 Net Profit / Operating Margin: ~$684,000

---

## Year 4: The Dominance Phase
**Primary Goal:** The "Card" ecosystem is the default dashboard for individual sports globally. Heavy B2B enterprise penetration.

### Projected Revenue: $3,300,000
- **B2C SaaS ($15/mo):** 10,000 total global athletes = $150k/mo ($1.8M annually)
- **B2B Coaches ($99/mo):** 1,000 total global coaches = $100k/mo ($1.2M annually)
- **Enterprise Contracts:** 10 Orgs/Pro Teams = $300k annually

### Projected Expenses: $1,000,000
- **Infrastructure & COGS:** $150,000
- **Personnel:** Scaled team (Engineering, Sales, Customer Success) = $650,000
- **Marketing & Advertising:** $200,000

### Year 4 Net Profit (Reinvested or Distributed): ~$2,300,000

---

## Key Metrics to Watch
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** The Lifetime Value of a B2B Coach must significantly outpace the cost to acquire them.
- **Churn Rate:** Ensure that physical goalies converting to purely software don't churn after a single season.
- **Enterprise Deal Cycle:** Pro Teams take 6-12 months to close. The B2C and independent coach tiers must subsidize this long sales cycle.
