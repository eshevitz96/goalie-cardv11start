# Goalie Card: Service to Software Pivot & Pricing Strategy

## Philosophy
Goalie Card is transitioning from a localized, service-based model (physical coaching hours) into an infinitely scalable software platform. The core pricing philosophy centers around **Leverage vs. Access**: 

- **B2C (Consumers):** Individual athletes pay for *Access* to developmental tools. Priced as a low-barrier monthly subscription ($15/mo).
- **B2B (Coaches/Orgs):** Professionals and Enterprises pay for *Leverage*—tools that save them time, organize their rosters, and help them scale their own revenue. Priced based on the value and scale of their business operations.

---

## 1. The Transition Phase: "Hybrid Retainer" 
*Target: Current local lesson roster*

Before moving purely to software, current clients accustomed to "buy-as-you-go" lesson packs should be transitioned to a recurring subscription that bundles physical access with digital tools.

- **Price:** $300 / month
- **Includes:** 
  - Complete access to the Goalie Card software platform
  - X amount of "Session Credits" automatically deposited per month (e.g. valid for 2-3 in-person lessons)
- **Why it works:**
  - Converts spiky, unpredictable income into Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR).
  - Forces athletic consistency (credits expire = athletes must schedule).
  - Anchors the value of the software. If they miss a physical lesson, they are still paying for the digital dashboard and AI protocols.

---

## 2. B2C: The Mass-Market Software Model (SaaS)
*Target: Goalies nationwide (and eventually other sports like Tennis, Golf, Racing)*

This is the pure-play software tier. No physical coaching is included. This model allows the business to scale infinitely without being capped by hours in the day or geographical constraints.

- **Soft Launch (Beta):** Free (Goal: acquire users, stress-test infrastructure, train the AI engine).
- **Version 1.0 "Pro" Tier:** $15 / month
- **Includes:**
  - Game/Practice scheduling
  - Journal entry tracking
  - AI "Expert Engine" analytics and custom drill generation
- **Why it works:**
  - Highly accessible price point for high school/college athletes.
  - Required 1,000 users to hit $15k/mo MRR (zero incremental time cost per user).
  - Serves as the Trojan Horse for sport expansion—once the core framework is validated with hockey goalies, it can be cloned for "Tennis Card" without the founder needing to be a tennis pro.

---

## 3. B2B: Independent Skills Coaches
*Target: Private coaches running their own small businesses*

Coaches need tools to manage their athletes, track development over time, and look professional to parents. 

- **Starter Coach:** $49 / month (Up to 10 athletes)
- **Pro Coach:** $99 / month (Up to 50 athletes)
- **Elite/Academy Coach:** $199 / month (Unlimited athletes + Assistant Coach logins)
- **Why it works:** 
  - B2B software is incredibly sticky; once a coach uploads 50 athletes to Goalie Card, they will never cancel.
  - It justifies its own cost: If Goalie Card saves a coach 4 hours of admin work a month, $99 pays for itself immediately.

---

## 4. B2B Enterprise: Clubs & Pro Teams
*Target: 3 Step Sports, St. Louis Blues, Red Bull Racing*

Enterprise organizations do not buy $15/month subscriptions; they buy customized infrastructure, security, and white-glove support. 

- **Club Organizations (Volume Focus):** $15,000 - $25,000 / year
  - Focus is on standardizing development across thousands of athletes and dozens of teams.
- **Pro Franchises (Margin Focus):** $30,000 - $50,000+ / year
  - Focus is on hyper-granular injury prevention, workload management, and elite performance tracking.
- **Includes:**
  - **White-Labeling:** The app is branded for the organization (e.g., "Blues Development Hub").
  - **Custom Integrations:** Hooks into their existing tech stack (Teamworks, Catapult).
  - **Data Sovereignty:** They own their data completely.
  - **Multi-Role Access:** Tiered logins for GMs, Medical Staff, Coaches, and Players.

---
 
## 5. The "Black Card" Tier: Elite Mentorship
*Target: Professional-track goalies, D1 prospects, and elite academy students.*

This is the pinnacle of the Goalie Card ecosystem. It is not advertised publicly but offered via personal invite to the top 1% of performers.

- **Price:** $900 / month
- **Includes:**
  - 24/7 direct access to the Founder/Elite Coach via the integrated dash.
  - Full game breakdown within 12 hours of upload.
  - Personalized weekly biometry and video feedback loops.
  - Included VIP access to select seasonal "Black Card" camps.
- **Why it works:**
  - High-ticket offering for low-volume, high-value clients.
  - Creates a prestige "aspirational" tier for the brand.
  - Anchors the value of the platform. If a user is paying $900/mo, the $15/mo SaaS looks like a bargain.

## Execution Timeline
1. **Launch Beta (Current):** Free for all. Accumulate users and find bugs.
2. **Launch B2C ($15/mo):** Turn on the paywall for new purely-digital users. Transition local clients to $300/mo Hybrid Retainer to stabilize base cash flow.
3. **Open Coach Tiers ($99/mo):** Once the B2C product is flawless, aggressively market to independent coaches as a roster management tool.
4. **Hunt Enterprise (Custom $$):** Take the validated data from 100+ coaches and 1000+ athletes to pitch massive custom deployments to NHL teams and mega-clubs.
